Hip & Knee Replacement • Revision Arthroplasty • Adult Reconstruction

Professional Biography

Dr. James A. Germano is a board-certified, fellowship-trained orthopedic surgeon specializing in hip and knee replacement, complex revision arthroplasty, and advanced reconstructive procedures. He currently serves as Chair of Orthopedics at Long Island Jewish Valley Stream Hospital, part of Northwell Health, and practices as a joint replacement specialist at Orlin & Cohen Orthopedic Group.

With more than sixteen years of experience in adult reconstruction, Dr. Germano is known for his meticulous surgical technique, thoughtful communication, and commitment to restoring long-term mobility for patients with degenerative joint conditions. He provides comprehensive care for both straightforward and highly complex orthopedic cases, guiding patients through every stage of evaluation, treatment, and recovery with clarity and compassion.

Patient Care Philosophy

Dr. Germano believes that exceptional orthopedic care begins with listening. He takes time to understand each patient’s goals, lifestyle, and concerns before recommending a treatment plan.

His approach combines evidence-based surgical techniques with a strong focus on safety, communication, and long-term outcomes. Whether performing a primary joint replacement or managing a complex revision, he prioritizes individualized care and works closely with patients to help them return to the activities they value most.

Research & Publications

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Germano is actively involved in orthopedic research, contributing to studies focused on joint replacement techniques, implant performance, and long-term patient outcomes. His work has been featured in peer-reviewed journals, professional conferences, and academic presentations.
