Research & Innovation

Dr. Germano is actively involved in clinical research aimed at advancing hip and knee arthroplasty, with a focus on robotic-assisted techniques, personalized alignment strategies, and enhanced recovery pathways. His work has been published in peer-reviewed orthopedic journals and referenced in clinical guidelines, contributing to the development of evidence-based approaches in modern joint replacement.

Through ongoing research and collaboration within Northwell Health, he continues to support innovation that improves surgical precision, long-term mobility, and overall patient outcomes.
