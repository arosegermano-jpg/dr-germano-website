Dr. James A. Germano

Dr. James A. Germano is a fellowship-trained orthopedic surgeon specializing in hip and knee replacement, complex revision surgery, and advanced joint reconstruction.

With extensive experience at Northwell Health and Orlin & Cohen, he focuses on delivering evidence-based, personalized care designed to restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Meet Dr. Germano

Why Choose Dr. Germano?

Dr. Germano combines advanced surgical training with a personalized, patient-centered approach to hip and knee care. As a fellowship-trained orthopedic surgeon with extensive experience in joint replacement, revision procedures, and modern technology-assisted techniques, he is committed to delivering safe, predictable, and long-lasting outcomes. His work within Northwell Health and Orlin & Cohen reflects a dedication to evidence-based medicine, collaborative team care, and continuous innovation—ensuring every patient receives the highest standard of orthopedic treatment.
